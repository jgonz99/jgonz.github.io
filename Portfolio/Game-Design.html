Deal or Scam

The Party Game

This is a party board game played with cards. It consists on sellers and buyers trying to sell or buy products respectively. The catch is that one of the sellers is a scammer so all the products he or she sells are fake or not real. Each player gets a role card and according to what they get they will try to buy or sell as many products as they can.

This was the first project on my Board Game Design Class. It was inspired on card version of Monopoly and was mostly illustrated by me and my classmate Tai-Shan Hopkins. Game Designers: Tai-Shan Hopkins, Greg Cohen and me. Besides being a game designer I also was the Project Manager of for the game.

The Escape Room

Primaries Under Attack!

The Virtual Version

This is the final project of my Board Game Design Class. Due to the COVID-19 situation, we were force to make an online version of a game, and we decided to keep the idea of the previous game but adapt it to a digital medium. So, the main story of the game is the same as the previous one, a secret organization called House of Anarchy is trying to ruin Democratic Primaries of 2020. The fate of democracy depends on the player, if he or she is able to win through all the puzzles they will save the elections. If not, 2020 elections are doomed! This was done in using Unity and the game designers are: Greg Cohen, Tai-Shan Hopkins, Caleb Dyson Marshall, Keita Takeda and me. Again, I took the role of Project Manager for this game as well.

Knock-out Agent!

Make a guess...

This was my very first game design ever. The gameplay is very simple. It’s just a computer guessing a number. In the game, you (The player) are the best hacker in the country. With your knowledge and computer skills, you have to help the field agent Scott Nickerson (computer). You are both part of a secret organization of spies that are in charge of the safety of the entire country.

In this mission you have to kidnap the CEO of NOLAN Technologies, who with his resources is trying to cause chaos in the entire world. The mission is to take him in custody but nobody can figure it out or he will fight back with his power and money. In order to do so, you have to tell agent Nickerson the exact number of ml of a knock-out drug he has to use in order to sedate him and take him in custody. If the agent doesn’t give him enough amount of the drug he will wake up easily and the mission will fail; on the other hand too much of the drug might overdose him and would end up killing him. To succeed, he needs to guess the exact number he has to give to this mischievous CEO. He has 5 chances to guess right the number.

Murder in a Shower

Build your own path

A Murder in a Shower is a storytelling game where the player makes decisions, and based on them the story takes different paths. This game is based on the movie Psycho, specially the iconic murder scene in the shower.

This game tells the story of Mary Harries, a woman who needs to get to an important meeting in the other side of the country. But she has trouble on her way there and finds herself having to spend the night at the Murphy Motel; a small and mysterious place right in the middle of nowhere… What she does not know is that if she is not careful, she might be walking into her own death. The player will be the character Mary, and throughout the game she will be offered the chance to defend herself by keeping some objects to attack with (knife). Finally, while she showers, she either dies or kills her attacker. Either way, a murder takes place in a shower.

Break-Tastic Four!

Block-breaker game

This is a “block breaker” game that consists on a player controlling a paddle using the mouse, to make a ball hit targets displayed on the screen. Once the player destroys all the targets he or she moves on to the next level or wins the game; but if the player drops the ball, he or she loses. This was a group project where I designed the second level, called “Grand Wizard”.

The game consists on a group of four superheroes that have to beat their enemies using their abilities and powers. The blocks are the villains that each hero has to defeat. The game consists of 4 levels, where the player can either pass to the next one in sequence or can select which superhero he or she wants to play.

Ship Defender

Alien Invasion!

This was also a group project and it was a shooting game where the enemies move in formations and shoot lasers against the player. The player has to destroy the enemy, protect itself and also the city below it. In this game I designed the first level. also did some original art work for backgrounds and other objects

The game consists Group of aliens invading earth and the player (the ship) has to protect it by shooting at every spaceship that appears on the horizon. If the aliens destroy every building in the city or the player itself, the player loses. If the player destroys all the aliens, he or she moves on to the next level or wins the game.

Rescue and Escape

Space Mission

This is a game that takes place in the future, where NASA sent a Spaceship on a rescue mission. The player is the spaceship and he or she has to launch from earth get to the Space Station, save the group of astronauts that are lost and get back to earth avoiding the aliens that get on their way. This was a group project where each member designed a level. The level I created was the second level called “Rescue Mission Part 2 (Inside).”

On the previous level, the Haddock Spaceship rescued the first half of the group of astronauts. On this level they have to rescue the second half, and go back to the space station, while avoiding to crash or hit the alien invaders. To rescue the crew they will have to unlock the door of the room they are located. In order to do so, the player has to land on the Platform Key A that will open the door. Once the astronauts are rescued, the player has to get to the End Platform. The player has to land on the Platform Key B to unlock the Second locked door and finish the level.

Dragon's Wrath

Rage Unleashed

This game takes place on the northern lands of the Medieval Europe. The Viking village of Kerg stole the egg of the dragon that lives on the top of the mountain. Now the mother dragon (the player) is furious and is going to unleash her wrath by destroying the village and taking back what it’s hers. The player has to fly from the top of the mountain to the viking’s castle, where he/she has to avoid obstacles and shoot fire to the vikings to destroy the village. This was also a group project where every member had to finish one level of the game.

I designed the last level which is called “A Mother’s Wrath”. On previous levels, the dragon has made its way through the mountain, lake and village on its quest of revenge. On this level takes place the final battle between the vikings and the dragon. Also, at the end the mother has to rescue the dragon egg when she gets to the castle. The player has to wonder around the different parts of the village to get to the castle while destroying as many houses as possible.

Witch´s Maze

Kill the witch!

This is a third person adventure RPG game that follows the story of a young hero trying to beat the evil witch. Ages ago, Kendra- the most powerful and evilest witch on earth- built a maze where she traps young kids to be devoured by her monsters so she can claim their souls and remain immortal. Now, in order to saves the souls of millions of kids you have to beat the witch’s maze and face her and her army of monsters. The player has to wonder around the maze, avoid being killed by monsters and pick up items that will help him/her beat the witch

I designed the game from the concept to the final details on the scenes. Assets were found on the Unity Asset Store, but the challenge was coding the game from scratch. I designed both levels and played a lot with the Timeline window in Unity.
