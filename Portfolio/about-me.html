Background

Where it all began

I was born in January of 1999 in Maracaibo, Venezuela. I was very fortunate to grow up with the most loving family that taught me values and skills that I now have. Ever since I was a little boy, I've been super 'geek' and 'nerd'. I always aced all my classes, and loved Disney, superheroes and videogames. I used to draw and play videogames all the time, and that's where I started developing my passion. When I was in High School, I did the International Baccalaureate (IB), which gives you a High School Diploma recognized throughout the entire world. One of those subjects was High Level Art, which required us to do an art exhibition. For that assignment I decided to create my own version of superheroes already known- like Superman and Wonder Woman- with a new greek mythology background. I did drawings, animations and 3D set-up of a fighting scene. During this assignment I found out what my passion is. Telling sotries through illustrations, animations or games.

After graduating with honors from High School in Venezuela, I decided I needed to go abroad to develop my passion further. I came here to the States in 2017 seeking to finish my Associate's Degree in both Animation and Game Design. This way I combine two things I'm very good and passionate about. Art and computers. Ever since I started college I can say that my skills have gotten way better with time, and I have expanded my skillset even more, learning new software and techniques.
