SAYONA

a short story...

3D animated student short film. Winner for the “Best animated short Film” of the 2019 DC South Asian Film Festival, hosted in Montgomery College. The short is inspired by the Venezuelan folk story about a gohst that hunts the souls of men who cheat on the spouses.

Witch's Maze

a fantastic
adventure...

RPG adventure game about a hero that has to go through a maze in order to fight an evil witch that has cursed the kingdom. This was my last project for my 3D Game Design and coding for gaming.

TUTU

an illustrated music video...

This is was a project for my video editing class, where we had to make a music video out of images and 'animate them'. The song is called Tutu, and the singer is Camilo Echeverry, a Colombian singer.

About me

Artist, Designer, Animator...

Hello! As you can tell, my name is Javier Gonzalez. I am 21 years old, and I am currently a Digital Animation and Game Design and Simulation student located in Maryland. I was born and raised in Maracaibo, Venezuela.

I moved to the U.S. when I was 18 to get my degree. Ever since then, I have been working on my art and skills very hard, and here is a little demonstration of the work I have done the past few years.
